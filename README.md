# Climate Monitor

**Decentralized Climate Intelligence: Real-time air quality monitoring powered by AI agents and secured by blockchain**

## The Problem

Climate data today is often unreliable, centralized, and lacks transparency. Citizens need trustworthy, real-time air quality information to make informed health decisions, but traditional monitoring systems are expensive and don't provide actionable insights. There's no decentralized way to access verified climate intelligence on-demand.

## Our Solution

Climate Monitor is a decentralized platform that combines artificial intelligence with blockchain technology to deliver verified, real-time climate intelligence. When you request air quality data for any location, five specialized AI agents work together to collect, verify, analyze, and provide personalized health recommendations - all secured and paid for through the Cardano blockchain.

## What Makes Us Different

- **AI-Powered Intelligence**: Five specialized agents analyze your climate data from multiple angles, providing insights beyond raw numbers
- **Blockchain Verified**: Every report is secured on Cardano, ensuring data integrity and transparency
- **Pay-Per-Use**: Only pay for the reports you need through decentralized micropayments
- **Personalized Health Guidance**: Get recommendations tailored to your specific health needs and activities
- **Multi-Source Validation**: Data is cross-referenced from multiple sources to ensure accuracy

## How It Works

1. **Enter Your Location** - Type any city name (e.g., "New York", "Mumbai", "Jammu")

2. **AI Agents Analyze** - Five specialized agents work together:
   - **Data Collector** - Gathers measurements from multiple trusted sources
   - **Data Verifier** - Validates data quality and detects anomalies
   - **Trend Analyst** - Analyzes 7-day patterns and predicts future conditions
   - **Health Assessor** - Calculates air quality index and health risks
   - **Report Generator** - Creates a comprehensive, blockchain-verified report

3. **Get Your Report** - Receive detailed insights in 2-3 minutes
